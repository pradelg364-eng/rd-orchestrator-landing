🔌 PLM Integration

Bidirectional sync with Teamcenter, Windchill, and 3DExperience. Knowledge assets, design criteria, and standards updates flow automatically between the intelligence layer and engineering workflows.

🤖 Autonomous AI Agents

Configurable agents for IP monitoring, technology scouting, and regulatory tracking run continuously. They detect competitor filings, market shifts, and emerging research — alerting your team before it's too late.

🔬 Knowledge ↔ Skills Bridge

AI extracts nano-skills from standards and LLCs to enrich the skill matrix. The skill taxonomy auto-identifies subject matter experts for peer review and mentoring assignments.

📅 Resource & Competency Planning

Demand vs. capacity forecasting across R&D programs. AI maps required competencies to available FTEs, flags bottlenecks before they become critical, and auto-generates training plans to close skill gaps aligned with the project roadmap.

📋 Design Review & Robustness

Structured gate reviews pulling DFMEA, test reports, Lessons Learned and design criteria into a single review package. AI-assisted robustness scoring tracks design maturity from concept to validation, with automatic ICD module sync and standards update flows.

📚 Unified Knowledge Repository

A single source of truth for Lessons Learned Cards (LLC), DFMEA, A3 Trade-offs, design criteria, codes & regulations. Peer-validated by SMEs, version-controlled, and semantically indexed — so no engineer ever starts from a blank page again.

🛡 Enterprise Security

On-premise deployment or private cloud. Role-based access, audit trails, and IP protection. Enterprise API partnerships with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google for state-of-the-art AI within your security perimeter.
